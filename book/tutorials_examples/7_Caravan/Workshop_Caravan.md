# Workshop Caravan

Welcome to the **eWaterCycle** Caravan workshop!

This is a workshop that is put together from the Climate Change Impact Analysis and the workshops from Africa.

First you will choose your Caravan catchment in notebook 1a, and then you will have to calibrate your HBV model manually.
In Notebook 1b we will show how we get ERA5 forcing for the chosen region.
2a and 2b are **optional** calibration notebooks! (they take a longer time to run)
Then we showcase how we get our climate data in 3a.
Which we then use to to run our calibrated HBV model in 3b
And finally we show a climate impact analysis.
